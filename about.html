We are Stackly Bio — where biology meets intelligence

Founded in 2020, we're an alliance of biologists, AI engineers, and medical thinkers building the infrastructure for next‑generation biotech.

01 · Who we are

Humanity + AI: the new era of bioengineering

Stackly Bio Technology is not just a platform — it's a movement. We combine CRISPR, machine learning, and deep biological datasets to shrink the time from discovery to cure. Our team includes former pharma leads, computational biologists, and data architects who believe that life science should be open, fast, and precise.

We started in a small lab in Boston, today we collaborate with 40+ research institutes across 12 countries. Our name, Stackly, stands for stacking knowledge, code, and biology — transparently.
